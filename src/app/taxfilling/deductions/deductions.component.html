Section 80EE - Interest on Home Loan

Deduction under section 80EE is available on interest on loan on your first house purchased. This purchase should be in the Financial Year 2016-17 (between April 1st, 2016 to March 31st 2017). The purchase price of the house can be upto ₹ 50 Lakhs and the total home loan can be upto ₹ 35 Lakhs.
